What is EverLaunch ?

7% of every transaction goes into a booster wallet where it is stored as BNB. Every 24 hours the contract gets forked and holders are airdropped a new token.
The team will burn 10% of the tokens from the migrating LP pair, and add the BNB from the previous day’s booster wallet which will create a higher starting price! Holders will be able to check the value of the booster wallet at any time on our website, and also see the projected next day’s starting price.
